During your intership, you will be assigned two projects within the first month. Completing at least one of these is essential to successfull continue your intership. if you're able to complete both, it will be considered a strong indicator of your commitment and performance, and will reflect positively on your overall intership experience.

Web Devlopment

Dynamix Networks provides intership opportunities in Web Devlopment, where a student learns to build and design website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, along with frameworks for frontend and backend devlopment. They gain skills in database management, API integration, and deployment, while also learning version control with Git/GitHub. Additionally, they develop UI/UX design sense, debugging, and testing abilities.
